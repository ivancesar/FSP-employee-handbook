WHAT WE DO

FarShore is a custom software development and design firm that views clients as long-term partners, not one-time projects. FarShore works with clients to build applications across web and mobile platforms and offers ongoing support for existing products. Whether you are looking to build the next high-impact ecommerce portal, integrate new software interfaces with existing hardware devices, or launch the newest engaging consumer mobile application, FarShore’s deep team has the knowledge and experience to be the ideal software partner for pursuing your software vision. Our vertically integrated model utilizes implementation specialists: web development, mobile development, graphic design, QA testing, project management and Dev Ops.

We take the time to understand our partners’ visions and then craft a team to execute product development and accelerate delivery. Our partners view us as a part of their core team and often look to us for recommendations that support their broader strategy.

Our Software Architects plan for and guide delivery of your development work streams. A manager from our dedicated architecture team takes on your project and serves as your personal resource for long-term guidance - technical roadmapping, feasibility analysis, and offline business strategy. Whether you are a Fortune 500 or a nimble startup, you can greatly benefit from the consultative support provided by one of our Software Architects. Each bringing multiple years of IT delivery experience, our architects go above and beyond in order to assure you’re moving in the right direction. Small companies often utilize our Architects as “Fractional CTO’s” - able to handle all aspects of your technical strategy. Larger companies engage the architecture team as an extension of their existing technology team.

We design user interfaces on all platforms - web, mobile, tablets, flash drives, and even shoes! Because we handle all design prior to development, our process keeps you involved and allows for rapid prototyping and iteration:

Design Theme Ideation. Overall feature set and concept is explored and in collaboration with our design team, you develop your theme and screen-by-screen outline.

Use Case Definition & Information Architecture. Largely completed by our design team and Architects, we examine the technical and customer-facing implications of design plans and determine the feasibility and effectiveness of previously ideated plans.

Low & High Fidelity Wireframing. Using computer models, our design team first creates a basic layout approximation in order to provide a rough visual of the product. Next, a more visualy representative prototype design is created and sent to you for an iterative review.

Original Artwork & PSD creation. Actual elements of the application are created in preparation for front-end development.

HTML/CSS or other front end technologies as appropriate. Visual-facing design is coded.

Cross Platform & Responsive Design-Coded design is optimized for key platforms.

PROJECTS WE ARE PROUD OF

Artifact Uprising

Everyone is a storyteller. Tell on. Featured on Mashable, Apartment Therapy, and NBC News, Artifact Uprising creates beautiful user-customized photo gifts out of its studio in Denver, Colorado. Artifact focuses on taking offline experiences and using online technology to enable their transformation into permanent stories. FarShore created a full custom Magento buildout for web and iOS to enable the ordering of custom photo products. With a fully dedicated team of eCommerce developers, testers, architects, and devops, FarShore continues to help the Artifact team tell its story. Artifact Uprising was acquired by Visual Supply Co. in early 2015.

Bucketfeet

Devoted to “creating super original footwear”, Bucketfeet is a Chicago-based shoe brand that collaborates with artists across the world. Through Dashfire, FarShore has led design, development and architecture of the e-commerce storefront since Bucketfeet’s launch in 2011. They have supported the growth of the company from a napkin idea to a global brand with millions of customers from around the world and art curated from their community of over 18,000 artists.

Apptuto

Apptuto is a gamified, social learning platform for Finance and Accounting qualifications. Despite being highly motivated to pass, the vast majority students studying for professional certifications, such as the Chartered Financial Analyst Program (CFA), fail. Apptuto has changed this, by gamifying learning and allowing people to study anywhere, anytime.

Apptuto’s focus is on increasing students studying efficiency. Apptuto is selling to Fortune 500 companies and is a scalable platform for other standardised courses. Apptuto is a member of the CFA Institutes Prep Providers Program, meaning their learning resources are up to date and that they follow the CFA Institute’s guidelines.

Over 3000 questions with detailed explained answers written by experienced tutors holding the Chartered Financial Analyst® designation. Apptuto’s Similar Question Generator allows you to generate new questions so you can practice a formula multiple times.
